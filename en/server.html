CHERNARUS VANILLA HARDCORE

1PP / HARDCORE / NO TRADER / MORE ZEDS / MORE WOLVES

IP: 193.135.10.252:20478

Chernarus Vanilla+ is a first person hardcore server. With us there are no traders, groups, chat, endless sprinting and all the rest of the bullshit that turns DayZ's survival idea into a farce. We have very little loot (about 50% of normal vanilla), storms, many wolves and bears, long and really dark nights ( NSG / NVG does not exist on the Chernarus.eu server ), Helicrash disabled, Zed hordes, many, many Zeds and some other funny things that make survival almost impossible. We are a pure survival server and believe me that's hard enough in this world everything is hostile. Experience and write your own story in the vastness of Chernarus.

Note: On our server, the Zed's can only jog and not sprint. We did this because of the huge number of Zed's we have on Chernarus.eu (about 5 times the usual amount at any location). At first we only had Walker in style from The Walking Dead but that was a bit boring so we decided to go with this solution. Watch your stamina because if you get your hands on it, it's the end of you.
