Rules

Rules for the Chernarus.eu Server

Note on the Rules:

Violating our rules may result in ban from the Chernarus.eu servers. Not reading the rules is no excuse as you will be made aware of it ingame. There is no right to play on Chernarus.eu

Behavior:

DayZ is a game...admittedly one that involves a lot of emotions...but it's still a game. We do not condone politics, religion, sexism, racism, insults, statements about age and gender or other shit talk.

Player vs Environment:

Chernarus.eu is a PvE server. This means that killing players is forbidden.

Base Raid:

Raiding bases is forbidden. On Chernarus.eu, a base is only a base or building that is locked. Everything else is not a base and can be looted. So make a little effort to secure your stuff.

Base:

Building a base is possible with the mod @Simple Base or with the vanilla system. Building inside buildings that are considered important locations for loot is prohibited. This includes all locations with Military, Medic, Wells and Police Loot.

Forming Loot Piles:

Forming loot heaps or storing them in a barrel or anything else to generate new loot is forbidden.
