Welcome Survivor

Hardcore has reached a new level!

Chernarus has fallen....

The Post-Soviet Chernarus is ravaged by an unknown virus, turning the majority of the population into crazed infected. The struggle for resources has created a hostile mentality among the survivors, driving the remnants of humanity to collapse. You are one of the few immune to the virus - how far will you go to survive?

DayZ is an unforgiving, authentic open-world sandbox online game where each of 60 players on our server has a single goal - to survive as long as possible, by any means necessary.
